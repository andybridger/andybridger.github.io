Working Papers & Research

Quantifying Service Trade Restrictions Using Modern Structural Gravity Models

MSc dissertation, commissioned by the Scottish Government · University of Edinburgh

Estimates the trade-cost impact of services-sector regulation using a theory-consistent structural gravity framework, recovering ad-valorem equivalents of behind-the-border restrictions to inform trade policy.

SSRN paper Policy paper (PDF) Estimating AVEs (PDF) Presentation

On International Uncertainty Links: BART-based Empirical Evidence for Australia

Bayesian econometrics project · University of Edinburgh

Applies Bayesian Additive Regression Trees (BART) to identify non-linear transmission of international uncertainty shocks to the Australian economy, comparing predictive performance against standard linear specifications.

Paper (PDF) GitHub repository
